Our Story

A genomics partner built for Indian research & diagnostics

TranscisAI was founded to close the gap between the pace of global genomics innovation and the infrastructure available to laboratories across India. We bring together sequencing hardware, automation, reagent science and bioinformatics under one roof, so labs spend less time integrating vendors and more time generating results.

Our approach is consultative, not transactional. From planning a new sequencing core to validating an NGS panel for clinical use, our scientists work alongside yours — configuring platforms, tuning workflows and supporting data interpretation at every stage of the genomics pipeline.

We work with hospitals, diagnostic chains, research institutes and biotech start-ups, helping each one build a genomics capability that is accurate, scalable and financially sustainable.
